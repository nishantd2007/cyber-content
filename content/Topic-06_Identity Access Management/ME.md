# Identity & Access Management: The New Security Perimeter

## The Password Problem That Won't Die

Despite decades of warnings, passwords remain the weakest link in cybersecurity.

- 81% of data breaches involve compromised credentials.
- The average employee manages 191 passwords.
- 51% reuse the same password across work and personal accounts.

Welcome to the identity crisis of 2026.

---

## Why Traditional IAM Is Failing

### Password Overload

Too many passwords to remember. Users choose weak, reusable passwords. Password managers help ‚Äî but aren't universally adopted.

### Phishing

Even strong passwords fail against phishing. Traditional MFA (SMS, email codes) can be bypassed.

### Privilege Creep

Users accumulate permissions over time. No one reviews or revokes unnecessary access. Former employees retain access for weeks or months.
